Extra-Curricular Activites

Women in Electrical and Computer Engineering (WECE)- This organization was created to recognize the women in the Electrical and Computer Engineering fields. There is not a large representation of women in these fields so the goal of this organization is to increase female presence. To increase the presence, WECE goes out into the community to interest young girls in engineering.
